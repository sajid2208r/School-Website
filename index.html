About Benchmark International School

Benchmark International School came into existence in 2022 with the motto 'Pursuit of Excellence in Education'. Founded by Chairman Mr. Zillur Rahman and CEO Mr. Kamruzzaman Jhontu with a team of experienced faculty members, the school is committed to providing global standard education that is affordable and effective.
